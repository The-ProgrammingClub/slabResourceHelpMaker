Your Introduction (Once upon a time)

It’s kind of like saying Once upon a time in a different and more specific way. Tell the readers what you're going to talk about in a few sentences or start off by asking a question or whatever else will help start off what you’ll be talking about.

Content (The Story)

  • Resources: talk about the resource and how people can use and who it’s most helpful for
  • Advice: If your sharing advice tells the story about how you came to that advice.
  • Tips and tricks: List them in a 1.) here's a tip > explain the tip 2.) here is a second tip > explain it etc.
